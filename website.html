Lamborghini is famous for its high-performance supercars, blending Italian design with cutting-edge technology. Founded in 1963 to rival Ferrari, it’s known for bold models like the Aventador and Huracán, featuring powerful engines and eye-catching designs. With scissor doors and luxury touches, every Lamborghini is a symbol of speed, innovation, and exclusivity.

Ferrari is a prestigious Italian luxury sports car manufacturer, founded by Enzo Ferrari in 1939. Known for its high-performance vehicles and rich motorsport legacy, the brand features iconic models like the Ferrari 250 GTO and LaFerrari. With its distinctive prancing horse logo, Ferrari symbolizes innovation, speed, and excellence in automotive design.
